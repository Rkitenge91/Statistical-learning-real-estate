# Hello!


Welcome to the webapp model demonstration for our 
***Statistical Learning*** final project.

## Instructions
Please use the sidebar on the left to navigate to our data demonstration page. You will be asked to input relevant property characteristic in order to estimate the **Price Ratio** and compute the estimated **Sale Price** of the property in question. 



Note that any outputs less than **0.5** or greater than **1.5** fall outside the range of our source domain and should be considered cautiously.
